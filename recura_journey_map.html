  • Clinic immediately loses confidence with no recovery path

  • Clinics don't know these modules exist
  • Feature adoption for non-core modules likely <30%
  • No built-in ROI summary at renewal time
  • Clinic can't quantify value — how many calls handled? Revenue recovered?
  • Renewal becomes a gut-feel decision with no data to support it

Calls or texts clinic number

Potential
Pain
  • Patient calls outside hours — no human available, unclear if AI is on
  • Patient sends SMS but doesn't know if anyone will respond
  • Patient realizes it's AI and immediately hangs up
  • AI greeting sounds robotic or too scripted — first impression fails
  • AI misunderstands intent and goes down wrong path
  • No graceful recovery when AI gets confused
  • No option to reach a human when needed
  • No suitable slots available — patient gives up
  • Booking confirmed but no immediate confirmation sent
  • Reschedule flow is unclear — patient doesn't know how to change via SMS
  • Cancellation is too easy with no rebooking prompt
  • Reminder SMS looks generic or spammy — patient ignores it
  • Confirmation reply not acknowledged — patient unsure if it worked
  • Payment link looks untrustworthy — patient doesn't tap it
  • No clarity on what they're paying for or how much
  • Payment page lacks clinic branding — feels like a scam
  • No fallback if patient wants to pay in person instead
  • No-show with no automated follow-up to recover the slot or reschedule
  • Clinic has no visibility into why patient didn't show
  • Follow-up sent too late or at wrong time — patient has moved on
  • Message is generic with no reference to what the visit was for
  • Survey is too long — patient ignores it
  • No acknowledgement after patient responds
  • No rebooking prompt in the follow-up — patient has to initiate themselves
  • Too much friction between "I want to rebook" and actually booking

Opportunity
  • Auto-reply to missed calls/SMS instantly: "We got your message, here's how to book"

  • Acknowledge confirmation reply with a warm auto-response

  • Keep survey to 1–2 questions max — prioritize completion over depth
  • Auto-share positive feedback to clinic dashboard
  • Embed one-tap rebooking directly in follow-up message
  • Use visit cadence data to suggest ideal next visit timing
